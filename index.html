How to play

Draw one continuous loop along the grid lines. It must pass through every dot.

Exactly two fence segments meet at every dot, so there are no loose ends or branches.

Every teal edge is a given clue and must be included in the finished fence.

A filled circle belongs inside a loop. A hollow circle must remain outside every loop.

When a puzzle asks for more than one loop, they enclose the same number of cells and never sit inside one another.
